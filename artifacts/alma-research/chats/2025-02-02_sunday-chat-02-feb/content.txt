i answer this one by one
1) spot down/gamma down means that as you go down on the chart MM gamma exposure decreases (shorter gamma, not necessary negative, but less and less gamma).
This means that they have to buyback less and less as price falls , so it meets smaller counterforce, expandin volatility.
they sell only, if it goes through a zero gamma line, but as you can see on the chart, there are multiple negative and positive gamma exposures. Dealers hedge their whole books, so we better look at it like what is the net gamma at some point. it is sitll positive above 5915
