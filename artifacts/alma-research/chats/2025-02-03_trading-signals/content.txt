Short vol trades?
there were some questions about the short vol trades (not touching the VIX complex now).

lets say you buy a 40-50 delta call 1x and sell 20-22 delta calls 3.5x (same expo)
by this you 1) long delta until the short call strike lvl, 2) net short vega, vanna and vomma, 3) long theta, long charm
This way you can capture the spot up until the short call strike (one can roll there, or close the legs), while simultaniously capturing premium from IV crush and time decay.
(opposite to the naked long vol directional trades), you have a directional bet.

Or simply short 20-22 delta puts to long 20-22 delta calls, in 2:1 ratio 30DTE, to net short vega and long theta.
With this, you essentially short the skew, but you are long vanna! So as vol declines, the breakeven also will slide lower, and also higher vol (making your delta higher) increases your PnL convexity as spot moves into any direction.
Also you are short charm, so your delta (you PnL sensitivity to spot change) will decrease by the time, while the long theta trade gives you premium.
However keep in mind, that you short theta of theta, meaning, your theta decreases by the time...
Also you're short vomma, so vol up/vega down, vol down/vega up, but keep in mind that your vega is negative (so vega up means less negative vega)
and also long veta, so by the time your vega will erode
