Without conception, without plan, without thinking, there is no longer-term growth.

You have $800 on your account. You go trading and you lose $300
Next day you win again $500
Next day you lose $200, but you get a successful 'revenge trading' and win $400

And finally on the last day you can gain $300.

Now listen...
 -300 + 500 - 200 + 400 - 700 + 300 = 0
