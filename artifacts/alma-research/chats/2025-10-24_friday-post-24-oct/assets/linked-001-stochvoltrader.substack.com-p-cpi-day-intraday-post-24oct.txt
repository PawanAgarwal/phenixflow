Subscribe           GEO RISK   We have new EU and US sanctions along with China tariffs…   EU first-ever bans LNG, phased—short contracts cease in 6 months; all Russian LNG imports to end by Jan 1, 2027. Also targets shadow-fleet tankers (port/insurance restrictions), third-country banks/crypto that aid evasion, and diplomat movement curbs. While US Treasury (OFAC) sanctioned major Russian oil companies and signaled stepped-up enforcement on energy revenue channels.    With the EU’s LNG phase-out locked in, Moscow will redirect cargoes to Asia and lean more on the shadow fleet; EU moves expand the list of sanctioned hulls and insurance/port access checks, raising freight & compliance costs.   Cost-push inflation is still not off the table.   However, the Fed is very much likely to QE in order to bail out the sentiment and the economy.
