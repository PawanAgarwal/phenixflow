this shot/long fly is based on the speed profile, netted out.
positive speed convexity = long fly
negative speed convexity = short fly.

speed has a time decay, just like gamma
for this time of analysis you really need the 3rd/4th order derivatives, and tons of datas.

the centroid is literally the centroid of the profile. thats the area or line where the upside and downside gamma effects affect the spot equally, and the intraday dealer hedging flow leaves 50/50 % chance for each direction. time decays are taken into account when calculating it. this is my propriotary equation.

for your Q, there is no short answer. read Nassim Taleb's book of "dynamic hedging" first.
