* Before the Sunday futures market opens, President Trump announces that he is exploring solutions.

* The futures market opens sharply at 6:00 PM EST on Sunday, but begins to lose momentum by the Monday market open.
* After the Monday market open, Treasury Secretary Bessent appears on television to reassure investors.

* Over the next two to four weeks, various members of the Trump administration create expectations for a trade deal.
* When President Trump announces a new trade deal, the stock market reaches an all-time high.

* This process repeats from step 1.
