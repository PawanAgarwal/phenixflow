2026-01-15T22:51:39.828Z | Alma

2026-01-15T22:51:56.338Z | Alma
dealer hedging flow

index-rebalance, closing auctions

2026-01-15T22:53:07.626Z | Alma

stocks move SPX
supply/demand + derivatives flows move stocks

2026-01-15T22:53:26.689Z | Alma
for example the futures

2026-01-15T22:54:32.068Z | Alma
the price of the futures and the value of the index are moving roughly together
(+futs have carry)

2026-01-15T22:56:44.938Z | Alma
if the futures move aways from the fair value, then arbitrage strats rebalance it:
futures goes above = the arbitrageur sells futs and buys cash basket
if futs goes below = the arbitrageur buys futs, and sells basket / SPY

2026-01-15T22:57:12.024Z | Alma

2026-01-15T22:57:28.464Z | Alma

2026-01-15T23:00:53.830Z | Alma

dealer becomes short delta,
he then buys ES futures.

ES moves first, cash basket lags, arbitrage opp, index catches up
