3) i like to get implied vol from VIX by myself. just like one does it with SPX. And it differs from VVIX approximately as much as VIX differs from SPX IV.
it is the 30 days IV of VIX. You get the next VIX options with expos T1, T2 bracketing 30 days. For each expo T you set a forward level Ft to the price of the matching VIX fut (discounting rfr)
now you build a strip, like... K0 is the strike lower/equal Ft closest to Ft
for each strike Ki you take mid quote OTM options (puts for Ki < K0, calls for Ki > K0; and at K0 using the avg of call and put.
now you use strike spacing deltaKi = (K(i+1) - K(i-1))/2, and you drop the dead wings (two consecutive 0-bid quotes,
now you can compute the risk neutral 30-day variance for each expiry using the standard VIX integral formula,
linearly interpolate the variance between T1 and T2 to a target of 30 calendar days, and as last step you annualize the sqrt

for VIX ATM, you can use Black-76 model (with the relevant VIX futures as underlying)
you pick the tenor, get the forward (VIX options settle to the VIX future, not spot VIX!!!), set the ATM strike close to 50d,matching VIX fut (same expo), then take the mid of the VIX call and put at this ATM and solve the Black76 backwards.
