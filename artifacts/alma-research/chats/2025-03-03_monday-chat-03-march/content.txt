Side note for the "up or down plp":
Market is not about up or down. One can trade theta, vega or/and delta.
My posts describe the characteristics so one can clearly set a trade, and can know what signs he should look at.
Bcs nobody knows if market will go up or down in the next sec on an intraday basis. but if we know the signs following from the characteristics, we can catch the momentums. And after we got the profit, we don't care about what happens after anymore. It is only a data for the next day to see how the momentum and the sentiment moves.
Stop thinking two-dimensionally.

Does anybody know how the market will behave right after opening? No. But we have lvls, we understand the characteristics, so we can wait the right moment patiently, and jump in when we see the signs we were waiting for.
