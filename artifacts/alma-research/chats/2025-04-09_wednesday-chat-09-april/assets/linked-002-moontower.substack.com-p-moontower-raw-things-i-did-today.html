my premise is the market has a weird theta component in it -- the longer we don't get a bullish development the more bearish the environment...my feeling is that theta is mirrored in the VIX backwardation. the longer we sit with good news, the more that future "drifts" up. don't get me wrong, we could justt stop moving right here, realized collapses, IV falls and the spot VIX comes down hard (although the future probably doesn't fall as fast...like any futures chain like oil the back months have less than 1 beta to the front month)
