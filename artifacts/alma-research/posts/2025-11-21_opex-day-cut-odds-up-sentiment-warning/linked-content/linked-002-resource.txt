This means that IV movements are amplified, and are very much sensitive. Net vanna is negative here, providing less resistance and less mechanical liquidity upward and supportive flows downward.Net zomma exposure is negative, so IV/RV correlation is more positive, but very low impact is expected.

The first two days are pivotal, even Tuesday close into Wednesday opening. The centroid should be taken out otherwise it will be even harder to climb up, and spot will be sucked towards the center of the fly. Veta and vanna will support upward squeeze towards the pivot, but only if the centroid is taken back.
