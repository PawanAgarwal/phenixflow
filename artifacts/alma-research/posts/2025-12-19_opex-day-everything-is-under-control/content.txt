Net speed profile remains negative, however this time, it is because of the heavy upside vol bid. They also bought downside vol, but it is only like 1/10 of the upside trade. Net gamma is flat-to-positive, upside vol of vol is sold. We also have an IC of 6715/20 - 6815/20; only some low local impact.

The centroid is at 6793.06 - 6792.28, this is a very important pivot now.

Upside pivot is at 6815.89with upside target of 6833.95Ambivalent, but it looks like this upside target would cause a reversion back to the pivot. Due to the upper wing of the IC, this pivot is sticky.

Downside pivot is at 6767.97 with downside target of 6715.68, again, aligning with the lower wing of the IC, that is sticky again.To break the downside pivot has a lower probability than to revert up from it back towards the centroid.

The most RV suppressing effect is clustering around the centroid area. This is not obvious or visible on the OD chart, you need speed profile to see that.So, the spot is set to range further, and market expects a positive drift. It doesn’t really bet on anything, as carry-trade uncertainity just came to the markets. We might get some good reversion trade opportunity, but still the odds favor for further vol selling. To break above the upside target, the AM session should catch a convex vol bid in order to go towards 6870. Low chacnce. Downside seems supported.
