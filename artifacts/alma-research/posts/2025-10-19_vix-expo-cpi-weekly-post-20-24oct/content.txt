The positioning draws a long fly pattern, centering around 6649.68It is net short vanna and speed, Long charm, long veta (if vol is supressed) and short color,long vomma and long zomma.

The distribution looks rather like a bet on downside stabilization downward, than a bet on an upside spike.Short vanna and short speed means, the bet is lower vol and higher gamma to the downside. Meanwhile long vomma is sold after the past weekly vol of vol spike, and now it is expected to manage any vol spikes. And long zomma controls the realized volatility (unlike vomma that controls the implied volatility). Vol up/gamma up/delta down = buying delta, but no acceleration.And vol down/delta up/gamma down = selling delta and acceleration.Long zomma is a typical trap for both sides. It makes risk premias mispriced into both sides. And this favors for momentum tradings and 0DTEs.
