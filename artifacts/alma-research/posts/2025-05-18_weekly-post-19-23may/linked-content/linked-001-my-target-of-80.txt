INTRADAY POSTCoding intraday positioning:

The speed profile drawns a short fly pattern at 5830/5900/5960Below 5894.97, the speed profile is very steep with a slope of 121.29%, and above this, it is negative with a slope of -51.10%; but the net profile is positive with a slope of 38.84%

This implies that i.e. in the shorter speed territory the intraday volatility is expected to naturally increase, but the ‘channel’ that it is moving within, is decreasing, vol upward grinds making lower highs, if the stabilizating interest is driving the spot. Also, if spot declines back down to the 5900, intraday vol should make lower lows. Why? Simply, bcs the positive speed profile is stabilizating to the upside. If this effect is in play when market is above the center of the fly, then local intraday vol should draw a declining channel. And if the channel declines steep enough, the spot can be pinned.Contrary, if the anxiety about these high levels is growing, the the channel should be ascending above 5900 (local negative speed), bcs positive speed means heavier left-tail risk. So, if you see intraday volatility realizing higher lows while decaying towards 5900, the gamma won’t be able to hold it, but it depends on the steepness of the channel. Especially bcs by the time that gamma will increase, and more vol will be needed to unclench.Below 5900, both local and the overall speed profile is positive, but the steepness is different!The steepness will decide if the local pin vs left-tail risk or the overall speed profile is in play or not.Because local speed is much steeper, the effect is way more stronger. if the local pinning forces are priced in, and customers are interested in pinning, you will see sharper decline in lower lows in vol, when market is going towards 5900, and shareper increase in hgiher highs, if it wants to unclench.But if no sharper decline in lower lows when spot approaches 5900, market will just go through that and grinding towards 5955; but in the afternoon, only higher vol can break through the 5900 gamma (due to positive color).
