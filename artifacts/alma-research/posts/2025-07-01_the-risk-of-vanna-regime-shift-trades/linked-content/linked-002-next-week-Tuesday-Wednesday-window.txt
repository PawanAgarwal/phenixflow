Reversion trading is a fast intraday “catch and run” directional scalping, basically.No long or short side. You trade what the market gives to you. If it goes to the resistance or reversion threshold, you go for a quick put, with SL, if it goes to the support lines, lower reversion bands, you go for a call with SL, small size. 55/60 delta ITM and ATM if you see the dynamic is lurking what you wanna see, bcs you want the most gamma to catch fast profits. After 20-30% profit you start to scale in, and you can roll your ITM calls back into 55 delta, when they reach ~80 deltas, if you see that the momentum is strong. You adjust the SL, take the profit, close the desk and go out enjoying summer. If the trade went against you (line is broken and confirmed through dynamics and pattern: retested and structure is built beyond the line), you take the small loss, don’t take it personal, just as the natural % when ppl lose on trading, and close the desk and go out enjoying the summer. You weren’t wrong nor right, market just did a different thing that you could not see forward… and mostly not foreseeable…
