GoldThe selloff wasn’t tied to one headline; it looked like a positioning/flow event after a nine-week uptrend. This was the steepest daily fall since 2020. In the week(s) prior, gold broke $4,000 (Oct 8), then $4,100 (Oct 13) and $4,200 (Oct 15), and 1M GLD IV ~30% with IV–RV ~12.5% was very rich, driven by a mix of rate-cut bets, geopolitics, and ETF/CB demand, a correction was inevitable. These drivers however didn’t disappear. I remain bullish on Gold until 3100/3200 zone holds. Late longs also can be squeezed out. 3700/3800 area are on the table, if 4k is weak now. Good buy-the-dip opportunity for long-term hold.

ChinaNew U.S. tariff actions on China are in play this month, alongside a separate USTR action that targets China’s maritime/logistics/shipbuilding ecosystem. Beijing has already counter-moved with port fees on U.S. ships (waivers favor China-built tonnage—this pushes ship routing & yard decisions and marginally tilts freight economics) and is litigating at the WTO, as predicted.Rare-earths/critical minerals tightness risk if counters escalate.Tariffs are a cost-push—headline effects first (goods/imports), with services less sensitive. Spring hikes already triggered China’s WTO case; a 100% rate would amplify pass-through unless offset by discounting/FX. Desks now price higher front-end equity vol on tariff days, and haven demand (gold, bills) persists even when equities rebound on “deal talk.”

INTRADAY POSTCoding today’s positioning:

Today we have a pretty asymmetrical short fly pattern centering around 6740, with net positive speed profile. This means that both tails are sold, but right-tail is way much havier. This setup primarly favors for ranging, but due to the significant asymmetry market can be magnetized to the upside. However, the slope of the profile makes it riskier.

The centroid is at 6762.46, functioning as a line in the sand for today, once the spot opens above or below it, it cannot be really broken (very-very low probability).the downside pivot is at 6732.36 from where the spot is supported until 6672.44 (given the shorter downside speed profile, the pivot is also a magnet, locally the strongest one).These levels can only be broken during the AM session. Into the PM 6705/10 works as a local pin if 6695 can be broken before 2.30PM, otherwise it is the 6670/85 zone.Keep in mind that the downside speed profile is very flat, and the gamma in this territory is net negative, so it is not as supportive as before. Market hedges the downside. This can provide us a nice reversion trade opportunity.If the 6672.44 line is broken because downside volatility catches a bid, we can see 6600 today as min.

Upside pivot is at 6775.37and above this point, the market has some freeway towards 6782.49 first, this is a very strong pin, the strongest one. If cal skews bids and realized vol catches up too, we can even see 6815, then reversion back to 6782 or 68036782 is the more probable one for EOD pin.

So if the spot chooses up, the positioning will hold it and stabilize it, supressing volatility. Good for premium collection strats, and some reversion trades with futures (no options), if the upper bounds are touched.If the market chooses down, we will see some momentum and reversion, tradable with options. After VIX suppresion yesterday, we can count on some buyback flow. Market prices 29% more downside than upside. But ranging, primarly, with downside movements.
