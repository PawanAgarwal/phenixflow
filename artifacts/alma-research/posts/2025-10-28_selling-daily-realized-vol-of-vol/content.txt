In the weekly post, I explained the interplay between short vanna and long zomma exposure and how it affects IV/RV correlation for the week. Now, what is interesting for me, is that intraday we can keep seeing upside realized vol and realized vol of vol selling, that reinforces the small effect of the weekly zomma supply. This is how the casino collects the chips from upside vol bettors. Because all those upside vols will be eventually dumped as reality checks in.

What is the purpose? Well, first collecting premiums from long vol 0DTE gamblers. And second, as we are in a ‘downside-hedge unwinding’ phase, plus we are at the gates of the buyback window, and like that the upside long vol bets increased. Squeeze up = risk, because volatility = risk. However, most of them don’t see the headwind, because it happens intraday, and it is not directly IV selling, but it is RV selling.This tells me, that the big players want stability. They don’t want upside risk to accumulate, especially trading at a momentum-wise pivotal area (see: weekly post).

Primary trend still constructive, but headline sensitivity is high (tariffs, sanctions, Fed). CTAs are net buyers but they are cutting, and remain so until 6640/30 are breaks.

Let’s look at today’s positioning…
