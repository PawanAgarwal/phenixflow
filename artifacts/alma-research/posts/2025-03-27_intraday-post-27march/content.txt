Here is the weekly post for context,and here is yesterday post where I described the potential algo/vol trap that is set to trap bears first then bulls into JPM expo.

Despite futs are red, realized vol is down by -1.89% during asia. The tariffs are not surprising, we knew about that, even in last year, they are all priced in. They will come and they will create volatility. The April OpEx week smells like very important.

Let’s look at the positioning and check today’s profile that can prove or disprove the estimated scenario:

You can see the MM long fly pattern at 5635/90/5730, that creates the rangebounding, sticky elevated vol environment. Its middle point is 5684.86 The speed profile is net flat-to-positive with a slope of ~0.04%The key pivot is 5755.42; because the speed profile is flat, vol matters a lot. Below this lvl, if the bears got momentum, the vol should expand and get realized intraday. Otherwise it disproves the trend, and the momentum is fake. But the real bear pivot is 5676.72, based on vol profile (8 pts below the middle of the fly, and 5 pts above the lvls that the HF algos are watching to buy based on deviation weighted avgs). Same with 5788.98 for the call side. Vol should decrease approaching this lvl, if the move has momentum to the upside. But the way the vol behaves at this point will confirm or disprove it. The crucial part is that the vol must be realized too. IV is not enough here.

The key point here is, that the flat speed profile makes it hard to see what they want. But vol reveals it. In positive speed, vol should decrease to the upside and increase to the downside (shorter spot/vol beta). And this should be realized, theoretically. Any deviation reveals the actual hand of the underlying flow.
