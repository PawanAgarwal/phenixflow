Trade the structure day by day, but be careful with chasing. The market is up on complacent Sept cut expectations due to the bad NFP while SPX/yields beta is highly positive. The key word here is “complacency”.

INTRADAY POSTCoding today’s positioning:

We have a local short fly pattern centered around 6350, but in regards of the ATM zones during the overnight session, we are in a local long fly pattern, with net short speed profile. This is a bet that market won’t go below 6350 today and will go upward, just as Trump promised… The centroid is in the 6360.44 - 6366.16 zone. This is crucial, bcs it separates the 6350 liquidity magnet from the small right-tail that the market is betting on. So today it will test if the call skew is bid or not, sentiment check.

The upside pivot is at 6412.51; and very importantly, market bets on liquidity there.However, to go up-there, is very unlikely, and market doesn’t bet on it too much. 6388 is a crucial pivot and seems very unbreakable, so the price is either above or below this line. Above, the 6405 line is vanna flip, very strong after 1.50PM, and will push the spot back down. Before 1.50pm it can be broken if spot starts to range at 6400 and volatility along with put skew is being sold, in this case 6427 would be magnet, double-top. 6400 is very weakly but magnetic.

Below 6388, we have 6372 level that is a weak support, but overall it will most likely go back down towards the centroid and pin to 6350. That is a strong effect. Convexity on call skew is needed to fight that.However after 2.30pm, if spot is still above the centroid, 6380 will be magnetic during the last 30 mins.

For any downside, you must see sudden unexpected headlines, but those will be managed too. Vomma selling is there both in a mechanical way and in a sentiment manipulation way.But pivot is 6325.84, with downside max of 6293
