INTRADAY POST Coding today’s positioning:

Well, market is literally long volatility into both ways, marking 6734.90 as the center of the fly pattern, that is almost symmetrical, speed profile is flat-to-positive, but it is like zero. Net gamma exposure is negative, charm is flat, so is vanna. So this position is like a coinflip. What I can mark, based on 0DTEs solely, is that 6622 cluster is a line-in-the sand sentiment-wise.

The centroid of the profile is at 6710.66 - 6713.69 area. The most important pivot.

Downside pivot is at 6596.19, this would break the structure, and would provide a hit to the WoR lows. I doubt we will hit it, but pressure is still there.

Upside pivot is at 6836.38, that is also very far from us now, but this would be the level where market would be out of the woods.

So rather I mark 6740/50 as a very important range, because above this line we can see realized volatility to calm down towards the upside pivot that I don’t expect to be broken today. Market does not expect that the 6780/6800 area will be broken, so if the flow take us above it, the sentiment will turn bullish fast. Also market hedges 6650 area, but does not expect 6622/10 to be broken today.

So all-in-all, the positioning is messy.Note, that the market positioning aligns with the upside area I marked in the free post, with high uncertainity, while market fears the downside gap.If volatility selling persists, that will be supportive above the centroid. 21.4 is a line in the sand for VIX, and you should see vvol up if VIX declines!A close above 6745 would be bullish.
