WEEKLY POSTLet’s look at the weekly positioning:

This is the Friday OpEx positioning.The speed profile is net negative, so is vanna, but not too much, and the fly pattern is a long fly around 6580 as center. The skewness of the distribution is right-tailish towards the center, but only with a small skewness of 0.2499 (so almost symmetrical distribution).At these volatility levels the positioning is net short veta but long vomma. This means, that vol exposure gets longer over time, into Wednesday, but it effects fades as we approach midweek and fully disappears into Thursday and Friday, while vomma works in the background forming a cap by dampening “longer vol” tendency for dealers if volatility grinds higher. In practice, this reduces the tailedness of the distribution, that is not too big anyway. The vomma supply implies a lower area of 6520.35 - 6490.12 and a higher area of 6635.79 - 6660.14

Zomma exposure is also positive (gamma vs vol relationship), that is expected in a long fly pattern… And zomma is significant. This means, if vol goes up, the upside gammas above the pivot of 6612.73 start to significantly increase (especially in the weighted cluster of 6692.46)and below the downside pivot of 6457.05 (with gamma cluster of 6402.21). This basically builds both upside and downside liquidity and caps the price action. Especially to the downside.

The main thing is that charm is net positive here, that produces a passive selling flow. The least charm is around 6511 (assuming constant vol level). And this also means that after the OpEx we can expect significant buyback flow!This only flips if price is within the 6445 - 6565 range and implied volatility is dumped below 9.26%, otherwise buyback flow remains into Sept 22.

The centroid range of this speed positioning is 6447.32 - 6505.26 area. Above this area, the upside gammas start to magnetize the spot towards 6580 and to the pivot, otherwise the centroid area is very much supportive.

The positioning is very much at the far right-side of the curve. Long zomma is a typical sign of it, bcs zomma is positive only at the very tails. This takes me to think that market expects market to top around these ranges I just mentioned. The reversion probability is at 92.14%, very rare signal and very high!But I expect time to reduce it a bit over the week.
