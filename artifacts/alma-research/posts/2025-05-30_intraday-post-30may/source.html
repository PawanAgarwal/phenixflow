INTRADAY POST
Coding today’s positioning:

This is a net positive speed position, but charm is flat-to-positive and vanna is also positive (this is because the local vol effect are stronger, see the 5850/5935/5985 long fly pattern on the chart)
However notionally the 5850 put gamma bar is very significant, where dealers are short huge amount of deltas. If those puts decay, dealers will sell. As the market goes upward, their short call deltas also will force sell. Upside is capped. 5850 is a potential pin.
The puts start to print below 5870.86; below this line we can expect local short speed effect (longer downside gamma, vol down).
To the upside, I code 5949.70 where vol is expected to start to decline, and 5975 would reject the price.
The centroid is the 5919.12 - 5937.12 range.
If downside vol catches up through outside influence, 5840 can be broken, then 5810 is next. If market stabilizes there, the 2 sigma move is the target. 5735 is hard stop.
